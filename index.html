About Me

Brandon Minard was once a young man, at the tender age of 24 he is taking a computer programming class. Due to his father being a programmer, he has grown up with progrommatic ideals being burned into his lackadaisical mind. For five years he worked QA at a company that has been banned from many European nations due to the sensitive nature of scholarly research and the differing ideals between them and the United States, and the ideals of freely presenting government funded research. His main interests are reading, video games, and sleeping. A long while ago he had aspirations, alas those have evaporated over years of inaction and a lacking commitment to much of anything. He wanted to be a writer at one point, and as such focused on being able to type grammatically correctly, and quickly. Not to mention his ability to stretch even the most simplistic of prompts over a great many lines.
